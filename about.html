About

Custom Bakes
Across Cape Town

From the grumbles of your tummy to the depths of her heart. Pam Cakes was built from a shared passion for baking and bringing people together through good food. Founded by Natalia Maliqua and Chelsea Poole, the brand started with a simple idea to create beautifully custom baked goods that taste just as good as they look.

What began as small, handcrafted orders quickly grew into a trusted name for cakes, cupcakes, biscuits, lamingtons, tarts, and cinnamon rolls across Cape Town.

Every recipe has been refined with care, every detail considered, and every order treated like it matters, because it does. Whether it's a celebration cake or a box of fresh biscuits, Pam Cakes is committed to quality, consistency, and flavour in every bite.
